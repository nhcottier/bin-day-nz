Support

Help with bins, reminders and council lookups.

Most issues are easiest to sort when we know the council, suburb and what the app showed.

Council lookup not working?

Council websites and data services can change. Include the council name, suburb, and the error message you saw.

Reminder problem?

Check iOS notification permission first, then send the property name, reminder time and iOS version.

Manual schedule help?

Tell us which bins should appear and the normal weekday or fortnightly pattern.

Open a request

Send a short support note.

Include council, suburb, iPhone model, iOS version and the steps that led to the issue. Do not include payment details.
